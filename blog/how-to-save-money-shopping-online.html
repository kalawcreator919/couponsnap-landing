Step 2: Layer Cashback on Top

Coupon codes and cashback are not mutually exclusive. Stack them.

Cashback apps pay you a percentage back after purchase, credited to your account. Top options in 2026:

  • Rakuten — 1–15% cashback at 3,500+ stores. Pays quarterly by check or PayPal. Install the Chrome extension so it activates automatically.
  • TopCashback — Generally higher rates than Rakuten for UK and US shoppers. Slower payout but worth it for large purchases.
  • Credit card portals — Many credit cards (Chase, AmEx, Citi) have their own shopping portals with bonus points. Log in through the portal before checking out.

Step 3: Check Prices Before You Buy

Price tracking tools reveal whether you're paying too much right now, or whether waiting a week could save you 20%.

Google Shopping Tab

Before buying from any single retailer, run a Google Shopping search. Google often surfaces lower prices from authorised sellers you wouldn't have found otherwise.

Honey's price history graph on Amazon is a legitimate tool even if you prefer other extensions for coupon codes. Open the Amazon listing, scroll down to the Honey section, and check if the current price is near a historical high or low.

Step 5: Never Pay Full Price at Cart Abandonment

This is one of the most reliable — and least-known — tactics: add items to your cart, then leave the site without buying.

Many retailers have automated systems that detect cart abandonment and email you a discount code (typically 5–15%) within 24–48 hours. This works best at mid-size direct-to-consumer brands that rely on email marketing.

Note: This tactic requires an account with the retailer and a valid email address. It doesn't work on Amazon or major marketplaces with millions of daily transactions.

Step 7: Join Store Loyalty Programs Selectively

Loyalty programs range from genuinely valuable (Amazon Prime for heavy shoppers, Costco membership) to barely-there email lists masquerading as VIP clubs. Be selective.

Rule of thumb: join a loyalty program only if you spend more than $200/year at that retailer. Otherwise the mental overhead of tracking points exceeds the value.

Best loyalty programmes with real savings in 2026: Amazon Prime, Walmart+, Target Circle, Sephora Beauty Insider, Nike Member.
